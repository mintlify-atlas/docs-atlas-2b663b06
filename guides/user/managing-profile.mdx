## Overview

Your user profile controls how you appear in the system and affects data filtering in some modules. You can update personal information, change your password, and manage your avatar.

## Accessing Profile Settings

<Steps>

### Navigate to Configuration

1. Click on **Configuración** in the main navigation menu
2. The system opens to the **Mi Perfil** (My Profile) tab by default
3. Your profile card displays at the top with your avatar and basic information

</Steps>

## Profile Overview Card

The profile header displays:

- **Avatar**: Profile picture (or initials if no picture uploaded)
- **Full Name**: As registered in the system
- **Email**: Your login email address
- **Role Badge**: Your assigned role (e.g., Preparador, Admin, Gerente de Calidad)
- **Branch Badge**: Your assigned branch (if applicable)
- **Admin Badge**: Red "Administrador" badge if you have admin privileges

<Note>
Your role and admin status can only be changed by system administrators. These settings control which modules and features you can access.
</Note>

## Updating Your Profile Information

<Steps>

### Open Edit Dialog

1. On the **Mi Perfil** tab, click the **Editar** (Edit) button
2. A dialog window opens with your current information pre-filled

### Edit Your Details

The edit form includes these fields:

**Nombre Completo** (Full Name)
- Your display name throughout the system
- Appears on batch records as "Preparador"
- Shows in reports and audit logs

**Puesto** (Position)
- Your job title
- Display-only, not used for permissions

**Sucursal** (Branch)
- Dropdown selection of available branches
- Affects data filtering in some modules
- Branch managers have this field locked to their assigned branch

**Área** (Department/Area)
- Your department or work area
- Used for organizational categorization

**Email**
- Your login email address
- Changing this requires email verification

<Warning>
**Email Changes**: If you update your email address, the system sends a confirmation email to the new address. You must click the verification link to complete the change. Until verified, you must log in using your **old** email.
</Warning>

**Contraseña** (Password) - Admin Only
- Leave blank to keep current password
- Enter new password to change it
- Must be at least 6 characters

<Note>
**Password Field**: Only administrators can change passwords through this interface. Regular users should use the "Forgot Password" link on the login page to reset passwords.
</Note>

### Save Changes

1. Review all fields for accuracy
2. Click **Guardar** (Save)
3. The system processes the update
4. You'll see a confirmation message
5. The page reloads to display updated information

</Steps>

## Managing Your Avatar

<Steps>

### Upload Profile Picture

1. On your profile page, hover over your current avatar
2. An **upload button** (camera icon) appears
3. Click to open the file selector
4. Choose an image file (JPG, PNG, or GIF)
5. The system uploads and displays your new avatar

<Note>
**Image Requirements:**
- Recommended size: 200x200 pixels or larger
- Square aspect ratio works best
- Maximum file size: 2MB
- Formats: JPG, PNG, GIF
</Note>

### Remove Profile Picture

1. Hover over your avatar
2. Click the **remove button** (X icon)
3. The system removes your picture
4. Your initials display instead

</Steps>

## Understanding Profile Fields

### Information Personal (Personal Information) Card

This card displays read-only view of your core details:

- **Nombre Completo**: Your full name as it appears on records
- **Correo Electrónico**: Login email address
- **Sucursal**: Assigned branch location

These fields populate automatically when you register batches in the Bitácora module.

### Rol y Permisos (Role and Permissions) Card

This card shows your access level:

**Rol Actual** (Current Role)
- Your assigned system role
- Determines module access
- Set by administrators

**Available Roles:**

- **Administrador** (Admin): Full system access
- **Preparador**: Can create batches and view own data
- **Gerente de Sucursal** (Branch Manager): Can view branch data and reports
- **Gerente de Calidad** (Quality Manager): Can view all quality data and reports
- **Coordinador**: Full report access
- **Director de Operaciones** (Operations Director): Limited access
- **Mostrador** (Counter): No production access
- **Cajera** (Cashier): No production access
- **Vendedor** (Sales): No production access
- **Director de Compras** (Purchasing Director): No production access

**Nivel de Acceso** (Access Level)
- **Administrador del Sistema**: Full access to all modules including user management
- **Usuario Estándar**: Access based on role assignment

<Note>
**Permission Summary**: Your permissions are automatically assigned based on role. You cannot change your own permissions - contact an administrator if you need different access.
</Note>

## Password Management

### For Admin Users

<Steps>

1. Click **Editar** on your profile
2. Locate the **Contraseña** field
3. Enter your new password
4. Leave blank if you don't want to change it
5. Click **Guardar**
6. You'll receive a confirmation message
7. Use the new password on next login

</Steps>

### For Regular Users

<Steps>

1. Log out of Quality Hub GINEZ
2. On the login page, click **"¿Olvidaste tu contraseña?"** (Forgot password?)
3. Enter your registered email address
4. Click **Send Recovery Email**
5. Check your inbox for the password reset link
6. Click the link and enter your new password
7. Log in with the new password

</Steps>

<Warning>
**Reset Email Not Arriving?**
- Check your spam/junk folder
- Verify you entered the correct email address
- Wait 5-10 minutes for email delivery
- Contact your system administrator if still not received
</Warning>

## Email Verification Process

When you change your email address:

<Steps>

1. **Submit**: You update your email in profile settings and click Save
2. **Verification Sent**: System sends a verification email to the NEW address
3. **Confirm**: Click the verification link in the email
4. **Activated**: Your new email is now active
5. **Login**: Use the NEW email for future logins

</Steps>

<Note>
**During Verification Period**: You must continue using your OLD email address to log in until you click the verification link.
</Note>

## Data Privacy

### What Information Is Stored

Your profile contains:

- Full name
- Email address
- Department/area
- Position/title
- Branch assignment
- Role and permissions
- Avatar image (if uploaded)
- Creation and update timestamps

### Who Can See Your Profile

**All Users Can See:**
- Your name (on batches you created)
- Your branch (on batch records)

**Administrators Can See:**
- All profile fields
- Your complete user record
- Your activity in audit logs

**You Can See:**
- Your complete profile
- Your own batch records
- Your own activity logs

### Profile Deletion

Only administrators can delete user profiles. When deleted:

- Your login access is revoked
- Your profile information is removed
- **Historical batch records are preserved** for traceability
- Batch records show your name as it was at time of creation

<Note>
**Data Retention**: Even if your profile is deleted, production records you created remain in the system for quality traceability and regulatory compliance.
</Note>

## Common Profile Tasks

### Update Your Branch Assignment

If you transfer to a different branch:

<Steps>

1. Contact your system administrator
2. Request branch reassignment
3. Admin updates your **Sucursal** field
4. Your data filtering automatically updates
5. Future batches are associated with new branch

</Steps>

<Note>
Batch records you created at the previous branch remain associated with that branch for historical accuracy.
</Note>

### Correct a Misspelled Name

<Steps>

1. Click **Editar** on your profile
2. Update **Nombre Completo** field
3. Click **Guardar**
4. Future batches use the corrected name
5. Historical batches retain the original name

</Steps>

### Request Additional Permissions

If you need access to restricted modules:

<Steps>

1. Note which specific features you need access to
2. Contact your administrator or quality manager
3. Provide business justification
4. Admin reviews and updates your role if approved
5. Log out and log back in for changes to take effect

</Steps>

## Troubleshooting

### Can't Edit Profile Fields

**Cause**: Some fields are locked based on your role.

**Solution**: Contact an administrator to request changes to locked fields.

### Avatar Won't Upload

**Causes:**
- File too large (>2MB)
- Unsupported format
- Network issues

**Solutions:**
- Resize image to 500x500px or smaller
- Convert to JPG or PNG
- Try again with stable internet connection

### Email Verification Link Expired

**Cause**: Verification links expire after 24 hours.

**Solution**:
1. Return to profile settings
2. Re-save your email change
3. Check for new verification email
4. Click link within 24 hours

### Changes Not Appearing After Save

**Cause**: Browser cache showing old data.

**Solution**:
- Hard refresh: Press Ctrl+Shift+R (Windows) or Cmd+Shift+R (Mac)
- Or log out and log back in

## Best Practices

### Keep Information Current

- Update your profile when you change positions
- Verify your branch assignment is correct
- Maintain an accurate email for system notifications

### Profile Picture Guidelines

- Use a professional photo
- Ensure good lighting and clear visibility
- Square crop works best
- Avoid logos or graphics

### Password Security

- Use a strong password (mix of letters, numbers, symbols)
- Don't share your password with colleagues
- Change password if you suspect it's compromised
- Don't use the same password across multiple systems
