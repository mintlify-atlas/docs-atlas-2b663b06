# Quickstart Guide

Get up and running with Quality Hub GINEZ in just a few minutes. This guide will walk you through logging in, creating your first production batch, and viewing quality reports.

<Note>
**Prerequisites**: You should have received login credentials from your system administrator. If you don't have an account yet, contact your Quality Manager or IT administrator.
</Note>

## Step 1: Login to Quality Hub

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to the application">
    Open your web browser and navigate to your Quality Hub GINEZ instance URL
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Enter your credentials">
    - Enter your email address
    - Enter your password
    - Click **Sign In**
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Verify your dashboard access">
    After successful login, you'll see the main dashboard with available modules based on your role.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Step 2: Create Your First Production Batch

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to Production Logbook">
    Click on **Bitácora de Producción** from the main dashboard
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Start the batch wizard">
    Click the **+ Nueva Bitácora** button to open the guided wizard
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Select product category">
    Choose your product category (e.g., "Limpiador líquido multiusos", "Detergente para ropa")
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Fill in batch information">
    Complete the required fields:
    - **Sucursal**: Select your branch location
    - **Preparador**: Your name
    - **Fecha de Elaboración**: Production date
    - **Código del Producto**: Product code (e.g., LIMLIM, TRALIM)
    - **Tamaño de Lote**: Batch size in liters or kilograms
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Record quality measurements">
    Enter quality parameters:
    - **% Sólidos M1**: First solids measurement
    - **% Sólidos M2**: Second solids measurement
    - **Temperatura**: Temperature in °C
    - **pH**: pH value (if applicable for product)
    - **Apariencia**: Visual appearance (CRISTALINO, OPACO, APERLADO)
    - **Color**: Product color
    - **Aroma**: Product scent
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Submit and view conformity">
    Click **Guardar** to submit the batch. The system will automatically classify conformity (CONFORME, SEMI-CONFORME, or NO CONFORME)
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Step 3: View Quality Control Dashboard

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to Quality Control">
    From the main dashboard, click on **Control de Calidad**
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Review your batch">
    Find your recently created batch in the measurement history table with conformity status
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Explore KPIs">
    Review the dashboard KPI cards showing conformity statistics
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Step 4: View Reports and Analytics

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to Reports">
    Click on **Reportes** from the main dashboard
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Select report type">
    Choose from available tabs:
    - **Calidad y Control**: First Time Quality (FTQ) analysis
    - **Análisis Comercial**: Production by family and category
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Apply filters">
    Use global filters to focus your analysis by date range, branch, or product family
  </Step>
</Steps>
