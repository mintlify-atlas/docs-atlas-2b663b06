## Overview

The product catalog contains all products manufactured and managed by GINEZ, organized into families and categories.

## Product Groups

Products are organized into 6 main groups:

### Cuidado del Hogar (Home Care)
- **Color**: Orange
- **Icon**: 🏠
- **Categories**: 
  - Aromatizante Ambiental
  - Especialidades cuidado del hogar
  - Limpiador liquido multiusos
  - Detergente liquido para trastes
  - Bases aromatizante ambiental
  - Bases limpiadores liquidos multiusos

### Cuidado Personal (Personal Care)
- **Color**: Emerald
- **Icon**: 💆
- **Categories**:
  - Crema corporal
  - Enjuagues capilares
  - Jabón liquido para manos
  - Shampoo capilar

### Lavandería (Laundry)
- **Color**: Blue
- **Icon**: 🧺
- **Categories**:
  - Detergente líquido para ropa
  - Suavizante de telas
  - Especialidades lavanderia
  - Reforzador de aromas (REFARO)

### Línea Automotriz (Automotive)
- **Color**: Slate
- **Icon**: 🚗
- **Categories**:
  - Automotriz

### Línea Antibacterial (Antibacterial)
- **Color**: Cyan
- **Icon**: 🛡️
- **Categories**:
  - Antibacteriales

### Productos Intermedios (Intermediate Products)
- **Color**: Indigo
- **Icon**: 🏭
- **Categories**:
  - Producto intermedio
  - Disoluciones intermedias
  - Producto terminado especial

## Data Sources

### Raw Materials (Materia Prima)
- **Source**: Google Sheets CSV
- **Environment Variable**: `SHEET_MP_CSV_URL`
- **Update Frequency**: Real-time on page load

### Finished Products (Producto Terminado)
- **Source**: Google Sheets CSV
- **Environment Variable**: `SHEET_PT_CSV_URL`
- **Update Frequency**: Real-time on page load

### Technical Documents
- **Storage**: Google Drive
- **Document Types**:
  - TDS (Technical Data Sheets)
  - SDS (Safety Data Sheets)
  - COA (Certificates of Analysis)
  - Labels

<Info>
The catalog is synchronized with Google Sheets on every page load. For production environments, consider implementing cache with periodic revalidation for better performance.
</Info>
